# Video Library

Curated videos that translate complex decisions into plain language. Always cross-check date and affiliation before sharing.

1. **"Ampullary & Duodenal Adenocarcinoma: What Oncologists Need to Know"** – ASCO GI 2025
   - Focus: subtype biology, systemic therapy sequencing, real-world toxicities.
   - Link: <https://meetinglibrary.asco.org/record/XXXX>

2. **"Managing Biliary Obstruction and Drain Care"** – Cleveland Clinic Digestive Disease & Surgery Institute webinar (2024)
   - Demonstrates ERCP footage, metal vs plastic stents, home drain maintenance checklists.
   - Link: <https://my.clevelandclinic.org/podcasts/biliary-management>

3. **"Molecular Tumor Boards for Rare GI Tumors"** – Foundation Medicine + MSK virtual MTBs (on-demand)
   - Case-based walk-through of MSI-H, HER2+, and KRAS G12C ampullary cases.

## How to use this library

1. Embed links into caregiver calendars before major appointments.
2. Watch talks at 1.25× speed and note timestamps for topics to revisit with clinicians.
3. Translate key lessons into the [Practical Playbook](practical-playbook.md) checklist to keep the care team aligned.
